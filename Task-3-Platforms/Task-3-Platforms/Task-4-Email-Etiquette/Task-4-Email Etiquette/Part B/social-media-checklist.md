# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
1. Use respectful and professional language in posts and comments.
2. Check facts before sharing news, screenshots, or forwarded content.
3. Keep your privacy settings updated on all social media platforms.
4. Think before posting anything that could affect your academic or professional image.
5. Use social media to share useful, positive, and meaningful content.

## Don'ts
1. Do not share personal information such as passwords, OTPs, addresses, or bank details.
2. Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content online.
3. Do not forward fake news, rumors, or unverified information.
4. Do not engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or unnecessary online arguments.
5. Do not upload anything that may damage your reputation in front of teachers, recruiters, or employers.
