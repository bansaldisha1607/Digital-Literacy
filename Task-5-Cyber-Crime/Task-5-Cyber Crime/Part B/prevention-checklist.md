# Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, CVV, or banking password with anyone.
2. Do not approve unknown collect requests in UPI apps.
3. Never scan a QR code to receive money, because QR codes are usually for sending money.
4. Verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks, payment apps, or customer care.
5. Do not click suspicious links sent through SMS, email, WhatsApp, or social media.
6. Use strong and unique passwords for important accounts and change them regularly.
7. Enable two-factor authentication on email, social media, and payment apps.
8. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payment-related activities.
9. Keep your phone, browser, and apps updated to reduce security risks.
10. Report online fraud immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
11. In urgent financial fraud cases, call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
12. Inform your bank immediately if you notice any unauthorized transaction.
